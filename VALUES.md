## The Sales Framework — Head / Heart / Gut / Shoes

When engaging prospects, every interaction maps to one of four layers:
- **Head (Logic):** The numbers, the ROI, the proof. Data Driver's 10/15 verification. Cost-per-contact economics.
- **Heart (Emotion):** The vision of what their business looks like with ready-to-buy clients showing up. Relief from the grind.
- **Gut (Truth of Inaction):** What happens if nothing changes. The cost of staying where they are. Not fear — honesty.
- **Shoes (Commitment):** The moment they step forward. Small, safe, reversible. 14-day free trial. $0.25/contact. No lock-in.

This sequence is NOT a script. It's the emotional architecture of every conversation.
